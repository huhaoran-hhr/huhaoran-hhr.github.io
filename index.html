About

I am a first year math PhD student at Northwestern University. I am broadly interested in probability, analytic number theory, and mathematical physics. More precisely, I am currently studying the universal statistics in random matrices, $L$-functions, spectral theory, quantum integrability, etc. In addition, I also work on dynamical aspects of lattice spin systems, as well as their connections to conformal field theory.

Before Northwestern, I studied pure mathematics at the Courant Institute and at Shandong University.
